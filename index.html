Project Idea

Overview

My project would be a centralised website for grad-program applications. The process of entering application after application can be incredibly tiresome and long winded. To cut out the re-entering of information, there should be a site that stores all your personal information/general question answers/resumes/cover letters/test results/Grades and automatically enter this information into grad applications. The site should alert users of relevant grad-programs like the Gradconnect website, but go a step further in terms of storing and automatically inputting your application information into the application. This means students will only have to enter data once and complete applications faster!

Motivation

The motivation came after my fellow students and I applied to 50 different graduate programs each with their own convoluted and difficult systems that involved hours of inputting data. The same information was required in each and every application with the exception of 1 or 2 questions; however the information never carried across. It made applications such a time consuming process and leaving many under graduates saying; THERE HAS TO BE A BETTER WAY!! If there was a quicker way to input the information, there would be more time available to apply to more jobs and therefore higher chance of securing a job!

Description

The stakeholders involves will be the students and the Companies looking for graduates.

The students would sign up to this page inputting email and linked in details so the programs are interconnected. The student will establish a profile that keeps a track of the applications that are active or interested in. The student would input all their data here including resume, cover letter, grades, work experience, generic information, and answers to some common application questions. This process will only have to complete once and never again.

The Companies that are looking for graduate will either sign up for this page or have to be recruited to join this page. The companies will also have a profile that can be updated with the status or progress of the process. Their graduate program information will be inputted onto the site and a direct link to the application process. On this application process there will be a button or pop up option to auto-fill your information as is utilised on paypal and payment pages. This will automatically fill out the application so it is simple as just updating a few relevant points and submitting.

Alongside this feature, there will be an additional feature that expands upon that of GradConnet and Whirlpool. There will be updates sent to students alerting of relevant graduate positions opening and warnings on closing dates as is utilised on GradConnect. There will also be a page on the site that provides a record of all the applications completed and has live updates on the status of that applications (e.g. if its opened, closed, or inactive).

Other uselful features may be discussion rooms for students, advertising spaces for company presentations and talks.
